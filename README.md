The goal of this dashboard is to answer key questions for job seekers and recruiters:

*   What is the distribution of company sizes hiring for data roles?

## üîç Key Insights & Findings
Based on the visual analysis:
1.  **Experience Dominance:** The market is heavily skewed towards **Senior-level** professionals (approx. 65%), indicating a mature market valuing experience.
2.  **Geographic Hubs:** The **United States** is by far the largest employer in this dataset (12k+ records), followed significantly behind by Great Britain and Canada.
3.  **Company Size:** The vast majority of data science roles (~92%) are found in **Medium-sized companies**.
4.  **Salary Outliers:** Specific niche roles like **AI Architects** command the highest salaries, with intermediate-level contracts reaching up to $800k in specific instances.
5.  **Global Disparities:** The choropleth map highlights significant salary disparities between North America and other regions.

## üìà Dashboard Features
The dashboard utilizes a dark-mode aesthetic for high contrast and includes the following visualizations:
*   **Global Salary Map:** A geographic heat map showing average USD salaries by country.
*   **Multi-Variable Scatter Plots:** Analyzing salary against experience level and employment type (Contract, Freelance, Full-Time).
*   **Donut & Semi-Donut Charts:** Visualizing the proportion of company sizes and experience levels.
*   **Detailed Matrix Table:** A granular look at average salaries broken down by specific Job Titles (e.g., AI Developer, Data Analyst) and Experience Levels.
*   **Interactive Filters:** Users can filter the entire view by **Job Title** and **Company Location**.
